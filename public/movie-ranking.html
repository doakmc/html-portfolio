The Best Movies According to Mazza

My top 3 movies of all time

Aliens

I was about 15 or 16 years old when I first saw this film, and I remember how totally engrossed I was. It blew me away. Absolutely loved the action and that feeling has stuck with me since.

The Blind Side

A truly excellent film with amazing performances from everyone in it. Based on a true story, Sandra Bullock deserved her oscar for her perforamce.

Star Wars - A New Hope

To be honest, any of the original three Star Wars movies could have been listed here, but the first one was ground breaking for it's time. A classic that I still happily watch with my son.
